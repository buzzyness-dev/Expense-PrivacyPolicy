Privacy Policy

Money Manage – Expense Tracker

Effective Date: 15 February 2026

Money Manage – Expense Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

If you sign in using Google Sign-In, we may access limited personal information such as your name and email address for authentication purposes.

Expense Data

All expense records entered in the app are stored locally on your device only. We do not access, upload, or share your financial data with external servers.

Usage & Device Information

We use Firebase services including Analytics and Crashlytics to collect anonymous usage statistics, device information, and crash reports to improve performance and stability.

Device or Other IDs

We may collect device identifiers and other non-personally identifiable information for analytics, crash reporting, and app performance monitoring purposes. These identifiers help us improve app stability and functionality. No personally identifiable information is associated with these IDs.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain app functionality
  • To enable secure Google authentication
  • To improve performance and fix technical issues
  • To enhance user experience
  • To show ads in the future if enabled, while respecting user privacy and Google Play policies

3. Notifications & Reminders

The app may send notifications related to:

  • Expense reminders
  • Important updates
  • Feature announcements

The app may use exact alarms to deliver timely reminders. You can disable notifications anytime in your device settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Information processed through third-party services is handled according to their privacy policies. If we add ads in the future, ad networks may collect anonymized identifiers (like Advertising ID) for personalized ads.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your expense data remains stored on your device. All data transmitted via third-party services is encrypted in transit.

6. App Permissions

  • INTERNET – Required for authentication, analytics, and future ads if enabled
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE – To check internet availability
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – To send reminders and updates
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED – To reschedule reminders after device restart
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM – To deliver time-sensitive reminders

7. Children’s Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here with a revised effective date.
